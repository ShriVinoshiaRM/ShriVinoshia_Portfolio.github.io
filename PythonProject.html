AirBnb Data Analysis

The project focuses on assisting Airbnb in finding the best deals to promote hosts in
New York City using a new social media ad campaign. It requires data collection, data cleaning,
and data analysis to help Airbnb decide which hosts will be promoted. Through this project,
I aim to enhance my skills in utilizing open-source data to make better business decisions.

Business Requirement 1 - Identify AirBnb hosts that are $100 per night or less, does not require more than a two night minimum stay, have atleast 100 reviews and are available at least 180 days per year ad campaign to promote

Initial data set

Removing rows that have empty fields

Dropping duplicate rows, filtering rows based on the business requirement and sorting results by neighbourhood group and last review

Grouping results by neighbourhood group and ranking by price to identify AirBnb hosts satisfying the business requirement

After analysis it has been found that the neighbourhood groups Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, Bronx and Staten Island are eligible to be promoted on the AirBnb ad campaign. Brooklyn (231 units) and Manhattan (143 units) are the two most popular neighbourhood groups of NYC.
